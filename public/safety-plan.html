Personal Safety Plan

A safety plan helps you prepare for moments of crisis by identifying steps you can take to stay safe.

If you are feeling overwhelmed or unsafe, consider the following steps:

  • Recognize warning signs (thoughts, feelings, behaviors).
  • Use coping strategies that have worked for you (e.g., breathing exercises, grounding).
  • Contact supportive friends or family members.
  • Remove means of harm from your environment if possible.
  • Reach out to a professional or crisis line (see helplines).
  • If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number.
